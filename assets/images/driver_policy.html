Privacy Policy

1. Eligibility

  • Drivers must be at least 18 years old.
  • Must hold a valid driver’s license and any legally required permits.
  • Must pass a background check and vehicle inspection (if applicable).

2. Code of Conduct

  • Treat all customers with respect and professionalism.
  • No harassment, abusive language, or discriminatory behavior will be tolerated.
  • Do not cancel rides/orders without a valid reason.

3. App Usage

  • The driver app must be used only by the registered driver.
  • Location access must be enabled during duty hours.
  • Do not use fake GPS or modify the app.

4. Trip/Delivery Rules

  • Always follow the app’s suggested route unless unavoidable.
  • Mark pickup and delivery properly through the app.
  • Report any issues immediately via app support.

5. Earnings and Payments

  • Earnings are based on distance, type, and applicable bonuses.
  • Payments are made weekly/monthly as per schedule.
  • Fraudulent activity may lead to forfeiture of earnings.

6. Safety and Legal Compliance

  • Follow all local traffic laws and safety guidelines.
  • Never drive under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
  • Ensure your vehicle is insured and maintained.

7. Data & Privacy

  • Location and trip data are collected to ensure safety and quality.
  • Personal data will not be shared unless required by law.

8. Account Suspension/Termination

  • Accounts may be suspended or banned for policy violations.
  • Fraud or repeated poor feedback can result in termination.

9. Support

  • Use in-app support or contact the helpline for assistance.

10. Policy Updates

  • Policies may change at any time.
  • Drivers will be notified of updates via app or email.
